Counselor at Camp Owatonna

As a cabin counselor, I was responsible for the well-being of all the boys in my cabin at all times. I served as a parent, role model, and friend in our nearly constant interactions.
As a waterskiing instructor I was trusted daily to drive and maintain very expensive and potentially deadly ski boats. I silmultaneously was tasked with teaching children how to waterski as well as constantly be a guardian of their safety.
I have been a Counselor at camp Owatonna nine weeks out of the summer for the past three summers. My overall experience as a camp counselor has given me a unique opportunity to develop skills such as leadership, planning, organization, responsibility, risk management, parenting, and many more. The requisite maturity and inherent responsibility involved in caring for other peoples' children 24/7 is something few other jobs require. As a result of this experience I know I will be one of the most diligent, organized, and aware candidates for any job I am qualified for.
